Perspective

I am Aryan Bhutani, a passionate photographer currently pursuing my B.Com at Shaheed Bhagat Singh College (SBSC), Delhi University. My practice is a seven-year odyssey exploring the intersection of mechanical majesty and human intimacy—from the soaring aircraft to the wild spirit of nature.

Diplomatic & High-Profile Documentation

My journey began in high-stakes environments, serving as the Vice President of Lenscraft Photography Club at Modern School, Barakhamba Road. During this tenure, I documented prestigious functions alongside the German Ambassador, several Union Ministers, and distinguished Armed Forces personnel, honing my ability to translate gravity into a single frame.

Institutional Leadership & Media Projects

Currently serving as the PR Sub-Head at Envision (SBSC), I lead visual communication strategies for the society. My professional portfolio includes technical documentation for Velox Media’s Cyber Suraksha podcasts and the Assam Ministry in Delhi, alongside providing creative direction for the official SBSC fashion magazine.
